Lasagna

Lasagna is my favorite dish

I really like lasagna because it has a lot of bechamele and meat

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Lasagna sauce
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Put the noodles into a bowl of hot water to soften them
  2. Prepare your meat with some tomato sauce
  3. Prepare the bechamele
  4. Assemble everything
  5. Put it in the oven for 45 min
